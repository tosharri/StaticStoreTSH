About Us

We are paddling people helping other paddling people! My name is Toni - I started paddling later in life but was instantly hooked! Before I even knew what I was supposed to buy, I began shopping! WOW!! Had a little sticker shock about how much the initial costs were to safety get on the water and begin learning and going on trips. After several years and many rivers, I now help people get over the hurdle of putting together the boats and gear they truly need and at a much better price! You shouldn't have to go to an outfitting store and buy everything new! Once you've chalked up some experience and start getting a better idea as to your preferences in brands and styles, we can help you transition! - we'd be happy to help you find the next 'must have' item on your list!

Paddling Outerwear and Clothes

Drysuits and Drytops are key to colder weather paddling - unlike wetsuits they are watertight because of the gasketing at the neck and arms but loose around the body to allow for thermal layering underneath when necessary.

Helmets, Foot and Hand Protection

Whitewater is created by water flowing over rock formations - when we're paddling our extremeties take the abuse! Protection for your head, hands and feet is key! Proper Fit, comfort and function will make a difference on having a good day on the river and an AMAZING day on the river!
